ok so

chemists in india upcharge the fuck out of you and nobody talks about it.

i got overcharged for something i could've gotten for 1/10th the price and it just sat in my head like that.

so i built agada.

scan any medicine strip, it tells you:
- if it's real or fake
- what the actual generic costs
- where to get the jan aushadhi version (literally 90% cheaper sometimes)
- if you even need a prescription for it (schedule H/H1/X)

no login.
no ads.
no paywalls.
just works.

took me way too long to build because turns out groq decommissioned their vision models and i had to figure out which ones still work. also their csv parser had a "const cur" bug that took me 2 days to find.

anyway.
try it: agadahealth.vercel.app

if you know someone who takes regular medication in india, send it to them. they'll save money.
